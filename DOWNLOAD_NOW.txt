STEP 1: DOWNLOAD THE DMG
  └─ Go to: /dist/
  └─ Download: J-System-0.1.0-arm64.dmg (161 MB)
  └─ Time: 1-2 minutes (depends on speed)

STEP 2: OPEN THE DMG
  └─ Double-click: J-System-0.1.0-arm64.dmg
  └─ It will mount (appear as folder on desktop)
  └─ You'll see "J-System" app and shortcut to Applications

STEP 3: DRAG TO APPLICATIONS
  └─ Drag: "J-System" app
  └─ To: Applications folder (also shown in DMG window)
  └─ Wait for copy to complete

STEP 4: EJECT THE DMG
  └─ Click eject icon next to DMG in sidebar
  └─ Or right-click → Eject

STEP 5: LAUNCH THE APP
  └─ Open: Applications folder
  └─ Find: "J-System"
  └─ Double-click to launch
  └─ Wait 5 seconds for startup

STEP 6: LOGIN
  └─ Use your email and password
  └─ Same credentials as website

STEP 7: ENJOY!
  └─ Beautiful modern interface
  └─ All your data synced
  └─ Start using!
