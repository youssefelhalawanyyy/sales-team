  └─ Drag "J-System" to Applications folder
  └─ Eject DMG when done

STEP 4: LOGIN
  └─ Use your same credentials (email/password)

❓ TROUBLESHOOTING

Q: App won't open?
A: First app load takes 5-10 seconds. Wait and try again.

Q: Can't login?
A: Use your same website credentials. Check internet connection.

Q: Data not showing?
A: Firebase might be loading. Refresh or restart app.

Q: Dark mode not working?
A: Click sun/moon icon in top-right of header.

Q: Pages loading slowly?
A: Lazy loading by design. First load is slower, then cached.

Q: Need help?
A: Check Firebase is accessible. Verify login credentials.
