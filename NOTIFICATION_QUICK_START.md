## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Where do I see notifications?
**A:** Bell icon (ðŸ””) in top-right corner of the app. Also as pop-up toasts.

### Q: Will I get notifications if I close the app?
**A:** Only if you enable Push Notifications in Settings.

### Q: How long do toast notifications stay on screen?
**A:** 5 seconds. You can also click to dismiss.

### Q: Can I turn off notifications?
**A:** In-app notifications always show. Push notifications can be disabled in Settings.

### Q: Why didn't I get a notification?
**A:** 
- Check browser is up-to-date
- Check if you have permission enabled
- Check if notifications are in your spam/junk
- Try refreshing the app

### Q: Are notifications real-time?
**A:** Yes! They appear as soon as an event happens.

### Q: Can I customize notification sounds?
**A:** That's handled by your browser/device settings.

## ðŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### Notifications Not Appearing?
1. Check if you're logged in
2. Refresh the page (Cmd+R or Ctrl+R)
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser

### Push Notifications Not Working?
1. Go to Settings > Push Notifications
2. Click "Enable"
3. Approve the browser permission
4. Check your browser notification settings
5. Make sure your device isn't in Do Not Disturb mode

### Getting Too Many Notifications?
1. Mark older notifications as read
2. Delete ones you don't need
3. Check if push notifications can be disabled in Settings

### Notifications Are Delayed?
1. Check your internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Check if browser is blocked from network access
4. Try disabling browser extensions
